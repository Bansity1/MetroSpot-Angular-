About

MetroSpot is a city-based discovery platform built to help people explore the best spots across Metro Manila — from local food spots to hidden gems tucked away in the city.

Mission

Cities are meant to be explored, not just visited. Our mission is to connect locals and tourists to the heart of Metro Manila and uncover the stories that make each district unique.
